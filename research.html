Research Areas

Innovating Human-Computer Interaction through Haptics & VR

Haptic Rendering & Perception

We focus on creating realistic tactile sensations in virtual environments. Our research covers texture rendering, stiffness perception, and vibration feedback algorithms to enhance the user's immersion.

  • ✓ Surface Haptics
  • ✓ Vibrotactile Feedback
  • ✓ Psychophysics of Touch

Immersive VR/AR Interaction

Beyond visual and auditory feedback, we explore how multimodal interaction can improve presence in VR/AR. This includes gaze-based interaction, 3D user interfaces, and locomotion techniques.

  • ✓ Multimodal Interaction
  • ✓ Gaze Tracking Applications
  • ✓ Virtual Locomotion

Accessibility & Assistive Tech

We develop technologies to assist people with disabilities. Our recent projects include sound-to-haptic substitution for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing (DHH) and accessible interfaces for mobile devices.

  • ✓ Sound-to-Touch Substitution
  • ✓ Accessible Mobile Interfaces
  • ✓ Tech for Social Good
